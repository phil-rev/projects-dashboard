# Factory Auditor

## Overview
Factory Auditor takes RevGravy's revenue factory IP and uses it to audit HubSpot instances. The tool:
1. Analyzes a client's go-to-market (GTM) motion
2. Predicts what factories should exist based on their GTM
3. Audits their current HubSpot setup against those expectations
4. Grades compliance and provides recommendations

## Key Concepts
- **Revenue Factories**: Repeatable, measurable business processes that convert opportunities into revenue
- **GTM Motion**: How a company sells (sales-led, product-led, hybrid, etc.)
- **Factory Design**: Based on industry, company size, sales model, and complexity
- **Audit Scoring**: Grades how well a HubSpot instance reflects the predicted factory architecture
